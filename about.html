About Me

I am a seasoned data analyst and proficient technical project manager with a robust background in healthcare. Armed with expertise in T-SQL, Python, and Excel, I specialize in spearheading project deliverables, and crafting insightful reports using Power BI and Crystal Reports.

Regular reviews of projects in Azure DevOps, coupled with sprint meetings prioritizing product backlogs, form the cornerstone of my project management approach. My daily interactions are client-facing, engaging with stakeholders to deliver transparent updates on project status through the lens of Scrum methodology, supported by my CSM certification from Scrum Alliance. My role extends across the team, collaborating closely with engineers to translate customer requests into detailed scope documentation while conveying technical information to non-technical audiences.

The opportunities I have been presented have allowed me to interact with different aspects of organizations. I play a pivotal role in responding to Request for Proposals (RFPs) and managing the entire Source to Pay (S2P) lifecycle, all in adherence to SOC 2 compliance and HIPAA standards. I am the go-to individual when it comes to conducting internal onboarding, as well as client trainings. With an experience in public speaking and presenting to stakeholders, I have also attended various trade shows and conferences as a technical specialist conducting product demos.

During audits, I contribute significantly by furnishing SQL-based reports to auditors at both state and federal levels, consistently maintaining satisfactory compliance ratings. When dealing with the inventory and distribution of controlled substances, every milligram is to be accounted for and tracked as it moves from clinics to patients. The thoroughness that the DEA demands in their reports has cultivated a baseline of nothing less than excellence and accuracy in my work.

My proficiency extends to categorizing information using data domains and mapping them into HL7 integrations. The commitment to data-driven decision-making is evident in the creation of comprehensive dashboards and reports. Prior to creating any visualizations, I develop and share best practices for code quality, versioning, repository, documentation, and data flows. Utilizing ETL processes, I ensure the seamless flow of data during large-scale transitions to cloud-based SaaS solutions. Additionally, I have hands-on experience configuring digital payments to facilitate "card not present" transactions, enabling seamless online transactions directly from webpages.


In essence, I am a versatile professional who thrives at the crossroads of technology, healthcare, and project management, ensuring the seamless integration of data, compliance, and innovation.
